---
title: "Learning in public keeps teams honest"
slug: "learning-in-public"
summary: "Share the messy middle, not just the polished launch."
---

When we narrate progress transparently — the unknowns, the tradeoffs, the upcoming experiments — we earn trust. I write weekly notes for clients that outline what we observed, what surprised us, and the decisions ahead. It keeps momentum visible and aligns everyone on what clarity we’re chasing next.
